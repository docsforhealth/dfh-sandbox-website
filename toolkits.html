  • Buprenorphine Basics

    • 10 videos
    • 50 minutes

    From receiving a buprenorphine waiver to prescribing buprenorphine on the ground, the Buprenorphine Basics Toolkit supports those new to prescribing buprenorphine with short video demonstrations of the knowledge and tools necessary for every step of buprenorphine use in a clinical setting.

  • Buprenorphine Deep Dive

    • 14 videos
    • 2 hours

    For practitioners who have had some experience prescribing buprenorphine on the ground, the Buprenorphine Deep Dive Toolkit presents evidence-based best practices and robust clinical and ethical reasoning scenarios for further refinement of buprenorphine practices.
