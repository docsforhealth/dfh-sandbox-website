    Pt is experiencing/at risk of homelessness or housing insecurity. Long-term consequences of homelessness include higher morbidity of both physical and mental health conditions, trauma and exposure to violence, and increased rates of ED visits and hospitalizations (Clark, RE.   • Explains effect of homelessness on specific outcomes

    Details

    Pt is experiencing/at risk of homelessness or housing insecurity. Access to housing has been found to significantly improve health outcomes, including decreasing anxiety and depression (particularly for individuals living with mental illness) decreasing number of children in poor health, decreasing rates of IPV, increasing food security, and decreasing healthcare costs (Bovell-Ammon, A. Health Affairs 2020; Taylor, L. Health Affairs 2018).

    Based on these findings, I recommended/discussed *** with pt.

    • Housing Insecurity
    • Food Insecurity
